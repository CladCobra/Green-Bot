Description

What Does this Product Do?

The Green-Bot is designed to make litter cleanup much easier. This is a major problem in many big cities, and even most small cities.

How is this Robot Different From Any Other Litter-Collecting Method?

We are aware that there are many other litter collecting solutions out there, but the Green-Bot has many benefits. For example, the Green-Bot is made out of recyclable/reusable materials, making it easier for cities to get their hands on one. Another benefit of the robot is that the robot will operate for around 16 hours a day and charge with solar panels for the other 8 hours.

How Does the Robot Collect Litter?

The Green-Bot will determine if the item picked up is trash or recycle using sensors & machine learning. Depending on the item, the robot will store it in either the trash or recycle compartment. When the compartments reach their capacity, the robot will head back to its “hub” and empty the compartments and restart this process.

How Does Charging Work?

The Green-Bot, as said before, will run for around 16 hours a day, and charge for 8. The robot will use solar panels to charge, so it is environmentally friendly.

Breakthroughs

A very important breakthrough that is needed for the Green-Bot is the improvement of machine learning. This is important for the Green-Bot as it needs machine learning to be able to detect whether an item is recycle or trash. Currently, teaching our robot to detech the differences between objects will take a while, and it will be a repetitive task, so to mass produce this robot, we will need machine learning to become easier to use.
